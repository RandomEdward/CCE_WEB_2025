What is CCE

The Chinese Cultural Evening (CCE) is an annual celebration of Chinese culture and Chinese New Year, created, led, and performed entirely by students. It represents a cherished tradition at UWCCSC (United World Colleges of Changshu China), with a decade of history and significance. CCE goes beyond being a student-centered event; it is an inclusive platform that welcomes participation from all. Last year, the global livestream of the event drew over 17,000 viewers, demonstrating its broad appeal and international reach. This year, the event is anticipated to attract an even larger audience, continuing its legacy of cultural exchange and celebration. This year, we celebrate the year of the snake, and our theme is looking forward to the future, while holding reverence to our past.

Who are CCE

The CCE Vision is brought to life by a dedicated core team of talented students who meticulously plan, organize, and execute every step of this extraordinary event. Our team of visionary directors stand at the very heart of what makes CCE special. They collaborate with our Zhixings, student-led clubs who specialize in traditional Chinese performance arts. From the dynamic exuberance of dragon dancing to the lethal beauty of Chinese martial arts and the enchanting tones of ancient Chinese music, these clubs infuse CCE with authentic cultural richness. Together, we create an intoxicating spectacle showcasing every facet of traditional Chinese art in all its glory.

CCE and You

We invite all students, parents, and faculty to join us on the UWCCSC campus as we dive into the festivities of Chinese New Year. We also host livestreams on various platforms, so keep an eye on your recommended page.
